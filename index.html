Offering a more natural alternative to their paraffin counterparts, beeswax candles draw upon the labor of our insect friends to create a candle that is long lasting and clean.

While more expensive than those of paraffin, beeswax candles offer a natural honey scent but can come with a wide array of fragrances, though they are generally yellow or white in hue.

When buying beeswax candles, particularly if you care about their natural element, make sure to check that they are 100% beeswax, as they may otherwise have additives.
